My name is Elahe Bagheri, a painter and visual artist from Iran. I hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Painting/Drawing from Soore University and currently work as an art educator, primarily with children. Teaching allows me to explore the transformative power of creativity, both in my own practice and by helping young minds express themselves through art.

From an early age, I have been drawn to the world of art, using it as a powerful means to express emotions and ideas that go beyond words. Fascinated by the interplay of colors, shapes, and details, I focus on aspects that others might overlook, allowing me to convey the complexity of human experience through my work. Art is my way of connecting, both with myself and with others, offering a window into the way I see and interpret the world around me.
